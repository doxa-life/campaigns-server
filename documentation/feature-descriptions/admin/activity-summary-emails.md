# Activity Summary Emails

## Overview

Activity summary emails give admins a periodic snapshot of key platform metrics — subscriber growth, prayer activity, adoption coverage, and engagement. They help the team stay informed about how the platform is doing without needing to check the dashboard.

## What's in the Email

Each email contains a table of 8 metrics with their current values and how they've changed since the last report:

- **New subscribers** — how many people signed up in this period
- **Total subscribers** — overall subscriber count
- **Prayer time committed** — total prayer time pledged by active subscribers
- **Prayer time recorded** — total actual prayer time logged
- **People groups with prayer** — how many people groups have active subscriptions
- **People groups with 144 subscribers** — groups that have reached the 144-subscriber threshold
- **People groups adopted** — how many groups have been adopted
- **People groups engaged** — groups marked as having active engagement

Changes are color-coded: green for increases, red for decreases, and gray for no change. Time-based metrics show changes formatted as hours and minutes (e.g., "+2h 15m").

## Frequency Options

Admins can receive summaries at four frequencies:

- **Daily** — sent every morning at 7 AM UTC, covering the previous 24 hours
- **Weekly** — sent every Monday at 7 AM UTC, covering the previous 7 days
- **Monthly** — sent on the 1st of each month, covering the previous calendar month
- **Yearly** — sent on January 1st, covering the previous calendar year

You can enable or disable each frequency independently. All four are enabled by default.

## Managing Your Preferences

Go to your Admin Profile page to find the "Activity Email Preferences" section. Toggle each frequency on or off. Changes save immediately.

Each email also includes a "Manage email preferences" link in the footer that goes to your profile page.

## Who Receives Them

To receive activity emails, an admin must be added to the stats notification group (configured in the superadmin panel) and have the relevant frequency enabled in their profile preferences.

## Current Limitations

- Cannot customize which metrics appear in the email
- Cannot change the send time (fixed at 7 AM UTC)
- No way to send a one-off summary on demand
